About

Where BIM meets the build

I'm Saheed Mohammed Aasik, a Structural BIM Modeler based in Sri Lanka with over four years of experience producing accurate, coordinated structural models for residential and commercial building projects — from G+5 to G+10 typologies.

What sets me apart is direct site experience. As an Assistant Civil Engineer, I coordinated structural drawings with live RCC works and conducted pre-pour reinforcement inspections — giving me an instinct for how models translate into real construction that pure desk-based modelers rarely have.

I work within ISO 19650 BIM standards, align deliverables with BIM Execution Plan (BEP) requirements, and specialise in Revit Structure, Navisworks Manage, and AutoCAD. Open to immediate relocation to Saudi Arabia, UAE, or Qatar.
